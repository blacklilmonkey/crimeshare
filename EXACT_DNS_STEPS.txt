🎯 EXACT CLOUDFLARE DNS SETUP FOR YOUR DOMAIN

YOUR RENDER URL: crimeshare.onrender.com
YOUR DOMAIN: [your-domain.com]

================================================================================
STEP 1: GO TO CLOUDFLARE
================================================================================

1. Visit: https://dash.cloudflare.com
2. Log in
3. Click on YOUR DOMAIN

================================================================================
STEP 2: ADD DNS RECORDS
================================================================================

Go to DNS → Records → Click "Add record"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
RECORD 1: Root Domain (yourdomain.com)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Type:      CNAME
Name:      @
Target:    crimeshare.onrender.com
Proxy:     ON ☁️ (MAKE IT ORANGE!)
TTL:       Auto

Click "Save"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
RECORD 2: WWW Subdomain (www.yourdomain.com)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Type:      CNAME
Name:      www
Target:    crimeshare.onrender.com
Proxy:     ON ☁️ (MAKE IT ORANGE!)
TTL:       Auto

Click "Save"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IMPORTANT NOTES:
✓ The "Proxy" icon MUST be ORANGE ☁️ (Proxied)
✓ If it's GRAY, click it to turn it ORANGE
✓ This enables Cloudflare's CDN and DDoS protection

================================================================================
STEP 3: SSL/TLS SETTINGS
================================================================================

1. Click "SSL/TLS" in the left sidebar
2. Set to: "Full (strict)"
3. Click "Save"

This ensures secure HTTPS connection!

================================================================================
STEP 4: WAIT FOR DNS PROPAGATION
================================================================================

⏱️ DNS changes take 5-60 minutes to propagate worldwide

✅ Once active, you can visit:
- https://yourdomain.com
- https://www.yourdomain.com

Both will work and redirect to your CrimeShare site!

Check DNS status at: https://dnschecker.org

================================================================================
VERIFY IT'S WORKING
================================================================================

After 10-30 minutes, open your browser and visit:
https://yourdomain.com

You should see your CrimeShare upload page! 🎉

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Issue: "502 Bad Gateway" or "404 Not Found"
→ Wait a few more minutes (DNS propagation)
→ Make sure Render service is "Live"
→ Check SSL/TLS is "Full (strict)"

Issue: Can't access site
→ Try incognito/private browsing
→ Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
→ Try from a different device

Issue: Cloudflare proxy not working
→ Make sure the cloud icon is ORANGE, not gray
→ Click the gray cloud to make it orange
