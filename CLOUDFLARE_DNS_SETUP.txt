🔥 CLOUDFLARE DNS SETUP - FINAL STEP
================================================================================

YOUR RENDER URL (Example): https://crimeshare-xxxx.onrender.com

================================================================================
STEP 1: FIND YOUR RENDER URL
================================================================================

Go to your Render dashboard:
1. Visit: https://dashboard.render.com
2. Click on your "crimeshare" service
3. You'll see your URL at the top (something like: crimeshare-xxxx.onrender.com)
4. COPY THIS URL - you'll need it for DNS!

================================================================================
STEP 2: CLOUDFLARE DNS SETUP
================================================================================

1. Go to: https://dash.cloudflare.com
2. Click on YOUR DOMAIN
3. Click "DNS" in the left sidebar
4. Click "Add record" button

Now add these 2 CNAME records:

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
RECORD 1: Root Domain (@)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Type:      CNAME
Name:      @
Target:    crimeshare-xxxx.onrender.com (YOUR RENDER URL)
Proxy:     ON (Make the cloud orange ☁️)
TTL:       Auto

Click "Save"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
RECORD 2: WWW Subdomain
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Type:      CNAME
Name:      www
Target:    crimeshare-xxxx.onrender.com (YOUR RENDER URL)
Proxy:     ON (Make the cloud orange ☁️)
TTL:       Auto

Click "Save"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

IMPORTANT: Make sure the CLOUD icon is ORANGE (Proxied), not GRAY!

================================================================================
STEP 3: SSL/TLS SETTINGS
================================================================================

1. In Cloudflare, go to "SSL/TLS" in the left sidebar
2. Set to: "Full (strict)"
3. Click "Save"

================================================================================
STEP 4: WAIT FOR DNS PROPAGATION
================================================================================

⚠️ DNS changes take 5-60 minutes to propagate worldwide.

✅ Your site will be live at: yourdomain.com and www.yourdomain.com

You can check if it's ready at: https://dnschecker.org

================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

If your site shows "404 Not Found" or "Invalid Host":
1. Make sure your Render service is "Live"
2. Check SSL/TLS is set to "Full (strict)" in Cloudflare
3. Wait a few more minutes for DNS to update

If you can't access your site:
1. Try: https://yourdomain.com (with https)
2. Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
3. Try incognito/private browsing

================================================================================
YOUR SITE IS NOW LIVE! 🎉
================================================================================

Visit: https://yourdomain.com (or your actual domain)

You can now:
- Upload files to your site
- Share download links with others
- Access it from anywhere in the world!
